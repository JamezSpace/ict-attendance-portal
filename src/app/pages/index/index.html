About RCCG DTCE ICT Department

RCCG DTCE ICT is the official digital innovation arm of the Junior Church. We exist to empower children and teens ministries with tools, strategies, and platforms that amplify Kingdom expression in a digital age.

Our work bridges the gap between faith and technology — equipping ministry teams with the skills and systems needed to thrive in a fast-evolving world. Whether through media, data, or digital infrastructure, our focus is clear: to enable scalable, Spirit-led excellence.

At our core, we are more than an IT department. We are digital missionaries — committed to raising a generation that serves God with relevance, creativity, and bold technological stewardship.
